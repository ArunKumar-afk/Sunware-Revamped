Keeping the Heat On: Intelligent Gas Demand Forecasting

Client Oil & Gas Giant
Capacity 6M+ Customers
Deployment 7-Day Forecasting
Outcome 98% Prediction Improvement

The Challenge

Our client, a massive large gas utility company serving over 6 million customers, faced significant challenges in predicting gas demand accurately. They required a way to forecast multi-day requirements—specifically for the next seven days—to optimize strategic gas purchasing decisions and operational planning across their vast distribution network.

Our Solution: ML-Driven Optimization

We developed an innovative platform utilizing advanced machine learning algorithms like Ridge Regression and XGBoost. The system centralizes data from daily sector-based gas usage and weather forecasts, preprocessing it via AWS Glue and Lambda. AWS SageMaker streamlines the optimization and deployment of these predictive models within the cloud stack.

Technical Stack

Ridge Regression
XGBoost
AWS SageMaker
Neo4J
Python
AWS Glue
PostgreSQL
React.js

Critical Features

Machine learning-powered forecasting not only predicts total demand but also detects anomalies in pipeline pressure that may indicate low or high pressure scenarios. The platform leverages AWS Athena for querying and analysis, and integrated root cause diagnostics to identify pressure issues within the multi-source data chain.

Project Conclusion

The resulting program serves as a critical strategic tool for millions of customers. By providing reliable forecasts, it empowers utility companies to allocate resources more effectively, proactively identifying pressure issues to ensure a consistent, efficient gas supply without operational disruptions.

Measurable Impact

Accurate multi-day forecasting (7-day outlook)

Proactive identification and resolution of pipeline pressure

Optimization of strategic resource management and planning

Enhanced data-driven decision making via real-time analytics

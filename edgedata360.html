About EdgeData360

Intelligence Where It Matters Most — At the Edge

EdgeData360 is a proprietary edge intelligence platform built by Sunware Technologies. It enables enterprises to collect, process and analyze data directly at the source — without relying on cloud round-trips — delivering sub-millisecond response times and smarter automated decision-making.

From IoT devices on factory floors to remote infrastructure monitoring, EdgeData360 brings enterprise-grade AI to the edge of your network — where speed, reliability and autonomy matter most.

Platform Capabilities

What EdgeData360 Does

A full-stack edge intelligence platform designed for the demands of modern enterprise operations.

Real-Time Data Processing

Process streams of sensor and device data at the edge with sub-millisecond latency — no cloud dependency for time-critical operations.

AI & ML at the Edge

Deploy machine learning models directly on edge nodes — enabling predictive maintenance, anomaly detection and intelligent automation without cloud latency.

Secure by Design

End-to-end encryption, zero-trust architecture and local data processing ensure your sensitive operational data never leaves the edge unless you choose.

Seamless Integration

Connect with existing IoT infrastructure, cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP) and enterprise systems through open APIs and pre-built connectors.

Unified Analytics Dashboard

Centralized visibility across all edge nodes — real-time monitoring, historical analysis and actionable insights in a single pane of glass.

Enterprise Scale

From a single facility to thousands of distributed edge nodes globally — EdgeData360 scales horizontally with your operations.
